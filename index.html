The Follower

Flora and Fauna

Flora and Fauna, our capstone project, is a story-driven 3D role-playing game designed to raise awareness about deforestation and poaching. As the 3D artist, I contributed by creating The Follower, an enemy that accompanies the first boss. The character was modeled in Autodesk Maya 2026 as a low-poly asset optimized for low-end devices, fully rigged and animated with five states (idle, walk, attack, hit, death).

This project demonstrates my skills in character animation, optimization, and gameplay integration.
